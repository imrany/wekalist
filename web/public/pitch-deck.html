knowledge management, privacy, and team collaboration with AI-driven insights

The Problem

Organizations are drowning in productivity tool chaos

  • Tool Fragmentation: Teams use 5-10 different productivity tools, creating silos and inefficiencies
  • Data Privacy Concerns: Sensitive organizational data scattered across cloud platforms
  • Limited Customization: One-size-fits-all solutions that don't adapt to unique workflows
  • Lack of Control: Dependency on external providers with unpredictable pricing and feature changes
  • Integration Nightmares: Countless hours wasted on manual data synchronization

Our Solution

Wekalist: Provide AI features like task generation, task summary and search, self-hosted productivity ecosystem

  • Collaboration: task collaboration in one seamless platform
  • Full Data Control: Self-hosted infrastructure keeps your data on your servers
  • Infinite Customization: Adapt every workflow to your organization's unique needs
  • AI-Powered Insights: Smart recommendations and automation that learns from your patterns
  • Open Architecture: Extensible platform that grows with your organization
  • Localization: We support different languages and local features
  • 24/7 support and updates: We provide full support of our service and software updates
